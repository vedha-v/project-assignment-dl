# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips

* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts and change them regularly
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important platforms like email and banking apps
* Do not click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments
* Always verify the authenticity of emails, messages, and websites before sharing information
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts
* Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank
* Always check the receiver’s name before approving any UPI payment


## Reporting Cybercrime

* Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
