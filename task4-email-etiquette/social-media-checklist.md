# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (For College Students)

##  Do’s

* Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments
* Use privacy settings to control who can see your content
* Share accurate and verified information only
* Think before posting, as online content can have long-term impact
* Use social media to build skills, network, and learn new things


##  Don’ts

* Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
* Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content
* Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
* Avoid spreading misinformation or unverified news
* Do not misuse social media during academic or professional situations
